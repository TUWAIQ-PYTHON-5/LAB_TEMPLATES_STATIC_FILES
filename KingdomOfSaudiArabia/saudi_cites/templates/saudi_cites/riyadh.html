About Riyadh City

Activites

Al Masmak Fortress

Standing at the gates of Al Masmak Fortress, you will witness the remains of an epic story of Al Riyadh conquest by King Abdul Aziz, may God have mercy on his soul. In your tour around the palace, you will be introduced to the traditional architecture of the city of Riyadh and back in time to witness the history of a new birth of the country.

Boulevard Riyadh City

The largest entertainment area in the capital city of Riyadh, with lots of virtual reality game experiences as well as international and local stores, in addition to numerous cultural theatres.

Riyadh Season

Visitors of Riyadh Season will enjoy a wonderful time in 15 different entertainment destinations all on the land of the capital, Riyadh. There is a variety of international events, musical concerts, fancy restaurants and many more.
