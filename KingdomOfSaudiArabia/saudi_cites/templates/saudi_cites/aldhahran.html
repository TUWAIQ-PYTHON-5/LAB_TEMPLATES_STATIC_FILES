The King Abdulaziz Center

The King Abdulaziz Center for World Culture or Ithra is a cultural center owned by Saudi Aramco, and it is located on the same site where the well of goodness, the first oil spring in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, was discovered in Dhahran. The design of the King Abdulaziz Center for World Culture comes in the form of a group of compact stones that include Among its flanks is a modern library, a center for innovation, an oasis for youngsters that is the first museum of its kind for children in the Arab world, a museum of natural history, art galleries, an archive center, and in its midst the knowledge tower that offers educational programs for pioneers of all ages. In 2018 it was chosen by the American magazine "Time" as one of the 100 greatest places in the world.

Energy Gallery

A list of tourist places in Dhahran is not complete without going through the details of visiting the Energy Exhibition Museum, which was dedicated to clarifying the details of the history of the oil and gas industry, in addition to presenting the technologies used in its extraction and production, all in conjunction with the opening of a library, an auditorium, and a 3D theater to explain the wonders of energy-related science.
